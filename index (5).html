Information About Company

Mr.Mohsin Hamadameen Bapir

Muhsin Hamadamin Babir, born in 1976 in Erbil, is the visionary founder and leader of Pongina Company. His journey in the construction industry began at a remarkably young age, fueled by a passion for building and structural design. With decades of hands-on experience, Mr. Mohsin has transitioned from a dedicated young learner into a seasoned expert, deeply rooted in the local market. His lifelong commitment to craftsmanship and integrity is the cornerstone of Pongina’s success, ensuring that every project reflects his personal dedication to quality and excellence.

Legal Status & Official Licensing

Pongina Company is a fully registered and legally recognized construction firm, operating under the official regulations of the Kurdistan Regional Government (KRG). We are formally licensed by the Ministry of Trade and Industry and the Ministry of Planning, ensuring that all our engineering and construction activities strictly adhere to national safety and quality standards. Our compliance with governmental legal frameworks reflects our commitment to transparency, professional ethics, and the highest level of accountability in every project we undertake.

Our History & Legacy

Pongina Company was officially established on May 6, 2009, marking the beginning of a journey defined by dedication and architectural excellence. Since our inception, we have spent over a decade and a half navigating the evolving construction landscape of the region, growing from a visionary startup into a trusted industry leader. For more than 16 years, our steady growth has been built on a foundation of reliability, high-quality engineering, and a proven track record of successful projects that contribute to the development of our community.
